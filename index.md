## 1. What is the most interesting topic covered in your domain this quarter?
The most interesting topic covered in this quarter was the immutability property of blockchains. This conecpt is interesting to me becuase it shows the core model of trust in decentralized platforms which plays into our project. Once data is recorded on a blockchain and verified, it cannot be altered or deleted without changing the entire blockchain's history. This is almost impossible due to the distrubited consensus mechanism. The permanence that blockchains provide is powerful for authentication applications like ours.
